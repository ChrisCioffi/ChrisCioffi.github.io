Welcome

My name is Chris Cioffi and I am a freelance journalist and master's student at the University of Maryland in College Park.

I have worked for newspapers in three states, including North Carolina's Raleigh News and Observer. I hope to pursue a career in Washington D.C. covering development topics.

I am an avid runner, who loves brewing coffee and craft beer.

Above you will find links to my social media, resume and a portfolio of my work. Catch me on Twitter or send me an email.
